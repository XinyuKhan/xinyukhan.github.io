离散系统的LQR推导

离散的LQR优化问题一般具有如下形式：

min⁡J=12xNTHxN+12∑k=0N−1[xkTQkxk+ukTRkuk]subject toxk+1=Akxk+Bkuk,x0given,H=HT≥0,Q=QT≥0,R=RT>0,(1)\min{J=\frac{1}{2}x_N^THx_N+\frac{1}{2}\sum_{k=0}^{N-1}{[x_k^TQ_kx_k+u_k^TR_ku_k]}}\\ \begin{aligned} \text{subject to}\quad&x_{k+1}=A_kx_k+B_ku_k,\\ &x_0\quad\text{given},\\ &H=H^T\geq0,\\ &Q=Q^T\geq0,\\ &R=R^T>0, \end{aligned} \tag{1} minJ=21​xNT​HxN​+21​k=0∑N−1​[xkT​Qk​xk​+ukT​Rk​uk​]subject to​xk+1​=Ak​xk​+Bk​uk​,x0​given,H=HT≥0,Q=QT≥0,R=RT>0,​(1)

优化的目标是已知xxx的情况下，通过选择控制输入 u0⋯uN−1u_0\cdots u_{N-1}u0​⋯uN−1​ 使得代价JJJ最小。我们将代价函数 JJJ 分开来看，定义从状态 xix_ixi​ 到 xNx_NxN​ 的部分代价函数为 JiJ_iJi​ ，除此之外部分的代价函数为 Ji′J_i^{\prime}Ji′​ ，于是有：

Ji=12xNTHxN+12∑k=iN−1[xkTQkxk+ukTRkuk]when0≤i≤N−1JN=12xNTHxN(2)\begin{aligned} J_i&=\frac{1}{2}x_N^THx_N+\frac{1}{2}\sum_{k=i}^{N-1}{[x_k^TQ_kx_k+u_k^TR_ku_k]} \quad\text{when}\quad 0 \leq i \leq N-1\\ J_N&=\frac{1}{2}x_N^THx_N \end{aligned} \tag{2} Ji​JN​​=21​xNT​HxN​+21​k=i∑N−1​[xkT​Qk​xk​+ukT​Rk​uk​]when0≤i≤N−1=21​xNT​HxN​​(2)

Ji′=12∑k=0i−1[xkTQkxk+ukTRkuk]when1≤i≤N−1J0′=0(3)\begin{aligned} J_i^{\prime}&=\frac{1}{2}\sum_{k=0}^{i-1}{[x_k^TQ_kx_k+u_k^TR_ku_k]} \quad\text{when}\quad 1 \leq i \leq N-1\\ J_0^{\prime}&=0 \end{aligned} \tag{3} Ji′​J0′​​=21​k=0∑i−1​[xkT​Qk​xk​+ukT​Rk​uk​]when1≤i≤N−1=0​(3)

显然有：

J=Ji+Ji′(4)J=J_i+J_i^{\prime} \tag{4} J=Ji​+Ji′​(4)

我们逐步对控制输入 uuu 进行最小化 JJJ 的操作。考虑控制输入的最后一项是 uNu_NuN​ ，有：

min⁡uN−1J=min⁡uN−1{JN−1+JN−1′}(5)\min_{u_{N-1}}{J}=\min_{u_{N-1}}{\{J_{N-1}+J_{N-1}^{\prime}\}} \tag{5} uN−1​min​J=uN−1​min​{JN−1​+JN−1′​}(5)

根据定义可知， JN−1′J_{N-1}^{\prime}JN−1′​ 与 uN−1u_{N-1}uN−1​ 无关，因为它表示的是 uN−1u_{N-1}uN−1​ 作用于系统之前的代价，于是有：

min⁡uN−1J=JN−1′+min⁡uN−1JN−1=JN−1′+min⁡uN−1{12[xN−1TQN−1xN−1+uN−1TRN−1uN−1]+JN}(6)\begin{aligned} \min_{u_{N-1}}{J}&=J_{N-1}^{\prime}+\min_{u_{N-1}}{J_{N-1}}\\ &=J_{N-1}^{\prime}+\min_{u_{N-1}}{\{\frac{1}{2}[x_{N-1}^TQ_{N-1}x_{N-1}+u_{N-1}^TR_{N-1}u_{N-1}]+J_{N}\}} \end{aligned} \tag{6} uN−1​min​J​=JN−1′​+uN−1​min​JN−1​=JN−1′​+uN−1​min​{21​[xN−1T​QN−1​xN−1​+uN−1T​RN−1​uN−1​]+JN​}​(6)

接下来在看 uN−2u_{N-2}uN−2​：

\begin{aligned} \underset{u_{N-2},u_{N-1}}\min{J}&=\underset{u_{N-2}}\min{\{J_{N-1}^{\prime}+\min_{u_{N-1}}{J_{N-1}}\}}\\ &=J_{N-2}^{\prime}+\underset{u_{N-2}}\min{\{\frac{1}{2}[x_{N-2}^TQ_{N-2}x_{N-2}+u_{N-2}^TR_{N-2}u_{N-2}]+\min_{u_{N-1}}{J_{N-1}}\}} \end{aligned} \tag{7}

根据上述规律，我们定义：

\begin{aligned} \hat{J}_i=\underset{u_i\cdots u_{N-1}}\min{J_i}&=\min_{u_i}{\{\frac{1}{2}[x_i^TQ_ix_i+u_i^TR_iu_i]+\underset{u_{i+1}\cdots u_{N-1}}\min{J_{i+1}}\}}\\ &=\min_{u_i}{\{\frac{1}{2}[x_i^TQ_ix_i+u_i^TR_iu_i]+\hat{J}_{i+1}\}} \end{aligned} \tag{8}

现在我们考察 J^N−1\hat{J}_{N-1}J^N−1​ 的具体形式：

J^N−1=min⁡uN−1JN−1=min⁡uN−1{12[xN−1TQN−1xN−1+uN−1TRN−1uN−1]+JN}=min⁡uN−1{12[xN−1TQN−1xN−1+uN−1TRN−1uN−1]+12xNTHxN}(9)\begin{aligned} \hat{J}_{N-1}=\min_{u_{N-1}}{J_{N-1}}&=\min_{u_{N-1}}{\{\frac{1}{2}[x_{N-1}^TQ_{N-1}x_{N-1}+u_{N-1}^TR_{N-1}u_{N-1}]+J_{N}\}}\\ &=\min_{u_{N-1}}{\{\frac{1}{2}[x_{N-1}^TQ_{N-1}x_{N-1}+u_{N-1}^TR_{N-1}u_{N-1}]+\frac{1}{2}x_N^THx_N\}} \end{aligned} \tag{9} J^N−1​=uN−1​min​JN−1​​=uN−1​min​{21​[xN−1T​QN−1​xN−1​+uN−1T​RN−1​uN−1​]+JN​}=uN−1​min​{21​[xN−1T​QN−1​xN−1​+uN−1T​RN−1​uN−1​]+21​xNT​HxN​}​(9)

代入 xN=AN−1xN−1+BN−1uN−1x_N=A_{N-1}x_{N-1}+B_{N-1}u_{N-1}xN​=AN−1​xN−1​+BN−1​uN−1​ ：

JN−1=12[xN−1TQN−1xN−1+uN−1TRN−1uN−1]+12(AN−1xN−1+BN−1uN−1)TH(AN−1xN−1+BN−1uN−1)(10)\begin{aligned} J_{N-1}=\frac{1}{2}[x_{N-1}^TQ_{N-1}x_{N-1}+u_{N-1}^TR_{N-1}u_{N-1}]+\frac{1}{2}(A_{N-1}x_{N-1}+B_{N-1}u_{N-1})^TH(A_{N-1}x_{N-1}+B_{N-1}u_{N-1}) \end{aligned} \tag{10} JN−1​=21​[xN−1T​QN−1​xN−1​+uN−1T​RN−1​uN−1​]+21​(AN−1​xN−1​+BN−1​uN−1​)TH(AN−1​xN−1​+BN−1​uN−1​)​(10)

对 JN−1J_{N-1}JN−1​ 对 uN−1u_{N-1}uN−1​ 求偏导数，并令其为0，有：

∂JN−1∂uN−1=RN−1uN−1+BN−1THAN−1xN−1+BN−1THBN−1uN−1=0(11)\frac{\partial J_{N-1}}{\partial u_{N-1}}=R_{N-1}u_{N-1}+B_{N-1}^THA_{N-1}x_{N-1}+B_{N-1}^THB_{N-1}u_{N-1}=0 \tag{11} ∂uN−1​∂JN−1​​=RN−1​uN−1​+BN−1T​HAN−1​xN−1​+BN−1T​HBN−1​uN−1​=0(11)

因此可以使 JN−1J_{N-1}JN−1​ 取最小的 uN−1u_{N-1}uN−1​ 满足如下必要条件：

u^N−1=−(RN−1+BN−1THBN−1)−1BN−1THAN−1xN−1=−FN−1xN−1(12)\begin{aligned} \hat{u}_{N-1}&=-(R_{N-1}+B_{N-1}^THB_{N-1})^{-1}B_{N-1}^THA_{N-1}x_{N-1}\\ &=-F_{N-1}x_{N-1} \end{aligned} \tag{12} u^N−1​​=−(RN−1​+BN−1T​HBN−1​)−1BN−1T​HAN−1​xN−1​=−FN−1​xN−1​​(12)

若要使其充分，还需满足：

∂2JN−1∂uN−12=RN−1+BN−1THBN−1>0(13)\frac{\partial^2 J_{N-1}}{\partial u_{N-1}^2}=R_{N-1}+B_{N-1}^THB_{N-1}>0 \tag{13} ∂uN−12​∂2JN−1​​=RN−1​+BN−1T​HBN−1​>0(13)

显然 u^N−1\hat u_{N-1}u^N−1​ 确实能使 JN−1J_{N-1}JN−1​ 取全局最小值。

将公式（12）式代入到公式（10），可以得到 J^N−1\hat J_{N-1}J^N−1​ 是一个关于 xN−1x_{N-1}xN−1​ 的二次型：

J^N−1=12xN−1T{QN−1+FN−1TRN−1FN−1+(AN−1−BN−1FN−1)TH(AN−1−BN−1FN−1)}xN−1=12xN−1TPN−1xN−1(14)\begin{aligned} \hat{J}_{N-1}&=\frac{1}{2}x_{N-1}^T\{Q_{N-1}+F_{N-1}^TR_{N-1}F_{N-1}+(A_{N-1}-B_{N-1}F_{N-1})^TH(A_{N-1}-B_{N-1}F_{N-1})\}x_{N-1}\\ &=\frac{1}{2}x_{N-1}^TP_{N-1}x_{N-1} \end{aligned} \tag{14} J^N−1​​=21​xN−1T​{QN−1​+FN−1T​RN−1​FN−1​+(AN−1​−BN−1​FN−1​)TH(AN−1​−BN−1​FN−1​)}xN−1​=21​xN−1T​PN−1​xN−1​​(14)

如果我们定义初始的 PN=HP_N=HPN​=H ，则上述关系变为：

J^N=12xNTPNxNJ^N−1=12xN−1TPN−1xN−1PN−1=QN−1+FN−1TRN−1FN−1+(AN−1−BN−1FN−1)TPN(AN−1−BN−1FN−1)(15)\begin{aligned} \hat{J}_N&=\frac{1}{2}x_N^TP_Nx_N\\ \hat{J}_{N-1}&=\frac{1}{2}x_{N-1}^TP_{N-1}x_{N-1}\\ P_{N-1}&=Q_{N-1}+F_{N-1}^TR_{N-1}F_{N-1}+(A_{N-1}-B_{N-1}F_{N-1})^TP_N(A_{N-1}-B_{N-1}F_{N-1}) \end{aligned} \tag{15} J^N​J^N−1​PN−1​​=21​xNT​PN​xN​=21​xN−1T​PN−1​xN−1​=QN−1​+FN−1T​RN−1​FN−1​+(AN−1​−BN−1​FN−1​)TPN​(AN−1​−BN−1​FN−1​)​(15)

将上述关系推广到更一般的情况，则有：

J^i=12xiTPixiFi=(Ri+BiTPi+1Bi)−1BiPi+1AiPi=Qi+FiTRiFi+(Ai−BiFi)TPi+1(Ai−BiFi)=Qi+AiT{Pi+1−Pi+1Bi[Ri+BiTPi+1Bi]−1BiTPi+1}Aiu^i=−Fixi(16)\begin{aligned} \hat{J}_i&=\frac{1}{2}x_i^TP_ix_i\\ F_{i}&=(R_i+B_i^TP_{i+1}B_i)^{-1}B_iP_{i+1}A_i\\ P_i&=Q_i+F_i^TR_iF_i+(A_i-B_iF_i)^TP_{i+1}(A_i-B_iF_i)\\ &=Q_i+A_i^T\{P_{i+1}-P_{i+1}B_i[R_i+B_i^TP_{i+1}B_i]^{-1}B_i^TP_{i+1}\}A_i\\ \hat{u}_i&=-F_ix_i \end{aligned} \tag{16} J^i​Fi​Pi​u^i​​=21​xiT​Pi​xi​=(Ri​+BiT​Pi+1​Bi​)−1Bi​Pi+1​Ai​=Qi​+FiT​Ri​Fi​+(Ai​−Bi​Fi​)TPi+1​(Ai​−Bi​Fi​)=Qi​+AiT​{Pi+1​−Pi+1​Bi​[Ri​+BiT​Pi+1​Bi​]−1BiT​Pi+1​}Ai​=−Fi​xi​​(16)

上述关系即是一般线性离散系统的LQR控制率。

现在考虑离散线性时不变系统，系统参数不再随角标变化，又考虑到PPP最终趋于稳态，则有如下关系:

P=Q+AT{P−PB[R+BTPB]−1BTP}AF=(R+BTPB)−1BPAu^i=−Fxi(17)\begin{aligned} P&=Q+A^T\{P-PB[R+B^TPB]^{-1}B^TP\}A\\ F&=(R+B^TPB)^{-1}BPA\\ \hat{u}_i&=-Fx_i \end{aligned} \tag{17} PFu^i​​=Q+AT{P−PB[R+BTPB]−1BTP}A=(R+BTPB)−1BPA=−Fxi​​(17)

上式中的第一项是 离散时间代数黎卡提方程 （Discrete time Algebraic Riccati Equation - DARE） ,第二项是状态反馈增益，第三项是最优控制率。

推导完毕。
