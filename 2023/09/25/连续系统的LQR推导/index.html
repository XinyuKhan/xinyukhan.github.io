连续时域上的DP（Dynamic Programming）

首先考虑如下形式的优化问题：

min⁡J=h(x(tf),tf)+∫t0tfg(x(t),u(t),t)dtsubject tox˙=a(x,u,t)x(t0)=x0m(x(tf),tf)=0u(t)∈U(1)\begin{aligned} &&\min J = h(x(t_f),t_f) + \int_{t_0}^{t_f}g(x(t), u(t), t)dt \\ &\text{subject to} \\ &&\dot{x} &= a(x, u, t) \\ &&x(t_0) &= x_0 \\ &&m(x(t_f), t_f) &= 0 \\ &&u(t) &\in \mathscr{U} \end{aligned} \tag{1} ​subject to​minJ=h(x(tf​),tf​)+∫t0​tf​​g(x(t),u(t),t)dtx˙x(t0​)m(x(tf​),tf​)u(t)​=a(x,u,t)=x0​=0∈U​(1)

其中tft_ftf​是终止时间，t0t_0t0​是起始时间，m(x(tf),tf)=0m(x(t_f),t_f)=0m(x(tf​),tf​)=0是终止条件（可能不唯一，因为mmm的值域是一个向量），U\mathscr{U}U表示对于u(t)u(t)u(t)的约束。

这个问题的解决的最终形式是一个非线性偏微分方程（Nonlinear Partial Differential Equation），被称作Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman方程（HJB），下面进行推导。

现在我们设[t0,tf][t_0, t_f][t0​,tf​]区间内的任意一个时间点ttt，我们考虑[t,tf][t,t_f][t,tf​]这个区间内的代价函数，其中τ∈[t,tf]\tau \in [t, t_f]τ∈[t,tf​]，那么有如下关系：

J(x(t),t,u(τ))=h(x(tf),tf)+∫ttfg(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ(2)J(x(t), t, u(\tau)) = h(x(t_f), t_f) + \int_{t}^{t_f}g(x(\tau), u(\tau), \tau)d\tau \tag{2} J(x(t),t,u(τ))=h(x(tf​),tf​)+∫ttf​​g(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ(2)

显然我们把区间[t,tf][t,t_f][t,tf​]分成两个区间来考虑：[t,t+Δt][t,t+\Delta t][t,t+Δt]和[t+Δt,tf][t+\Delta t,t_f][t+Δt,tf​]。如下：

J^(x(t),t)=min⁡u(τ)∈U,τ∈[t,tf]J(x(t),t,u(τ))=min⁡u(τ)∈U,τ∈[t,tf]{h(x(tf),tf)+∫ttfg(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ}=min⁡u(τ)∈U,τ∈[t,tf]{h(x(tf),tf)+∫tt+Δtg(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ+∫t+Δttfg(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ}(4)\begin{aligned} \hat{J}(x(t), t) &= \underset{u(\tau)\in\mathscr{U},\tau\in[t, t_f]}{\min}J(x(t),t,u(\tau)) \\ &=\underset{u(\tau)\in\mathscr{U},\tau\in[t, t_f]}{\min}\left\{h(x(t_f), t_f)+\int_{t}^{t_f}g(x(\tau),u(\tau), \tau)d\tau\right\}\\ &=\underset{u(\tau)\in\mathscr{U},\tau\in[t, t_f]}{\min}\left\{h(x(t_f),t_f)+\int_{t}^{t+\Delta{t}}g(x(\tau), u(\tau),\tau)d\tau+\int_{t+\Delta{t}}^{t_f}g(x(\tau), u(\tau),\tau)d\tau\right\} \end{aligned} \tag{4} J^(x(t),t)​=u(τ)∈U,τ∈[t,tf​]min​J(x(t),t,u(τ))=u(τ)∈U,τ∈[t,tf​]min​{h(x(tf​),tf​)+∫ttf​​g(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ}=u(τ)∈U,τ∈[t,tf​]min​{h(x(tf​),tf​)+∫tt+Δt​g(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ+∫t+Δttf​​g(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ}​(4)

我们定义[t+Δt,tf][t+\Delta t,t_f][t+Δt,tf​]范围内的最优代价函数：

J^(x(t+Δt),t+Δt)=min⁡u(τ)∈U,τ∈[t+Δt,tf]{h(x(tf),tf)+∫t+Δttfg(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ}(5)\begin{aligned} \hat{J}(x(t+\Delta{t}), t+\Delta{t}) &=\underset{u(\tau)\in\mathscr{U},\tau\in[t+\Delta{t}, t_f]}{\min}\left\{h(x(t_f),t_f)+\int_{t+\Delta{t}}^{t_f}g(x(\tau), u(\tau),\tau)d\tau\right\} \end{aligned} \tag{5} J^(x(t+Δt),t+Δt)​=u(τ)∈U,τ∈[t+Δt,tf​]min​{h(x(tf​),tf​)+∫t+Δttf​​g(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ}​(5)

于是有：

J^(x(t),t)=min⁡u(τ)∈U,τ∈[t,t+Δt]{∫tt+Δtg(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ+J^(x(t+Δt),t+Δt)}(6)\begin{aligned} \hat{J}(x(t), t) &=\underset{u(\tau)\in\mathscr{U},\tau\in[t, t+\Delta{t}]}{\min}\left\{\int_{t}^{t+\Delta{t}}g(x(\tau), u(\tau),\tau)d\tau+\hat{J}(x(t+\Delta{t}), t+\Delta{t}) \right\} \end{aligned} \tag{6} J^(x(t),t)​=u(τ)∈U,τ∈[t,t+Δt]min​{∫tt+Δt​g(x(τ),u(τ),τ)dτ+J^(x(t+Δt),t+Δt)}​(6)

我们假设Δt\Delta tΔt很小，那么可以对上式进行泰勒展开，有：

J^(x(t+Δt),t+Δt)≈J^(x(t),t)+[∂J^∂t(x(t),t)]Δt+[∂J^∂x(x(t),t)][x(t+Δt)−x(t)](7)\hat{J}(x(t+\Delta{t}), t+\Delta{t}) \approx\hat{J}(x(t), t)+\left[\frac{\partial\hat{J}}{\partial{t}}(x(t),t)\right]\Delta{t}+\left[\frac{\partial\hat{J}}{\partial{x}}(x(t),t)\right][x(t+\Delta{t})-x(t)] \tag{7} J^(x(t+Δt),t+Δt)≈J^(x(t),t)+[∂t∂J^​(x(t),t)]Δt+[∂x∂J^​(x(t),t)][x(t+Δt)−x(t)](7)

下面定义其中两个偏微分的别名：

J^t(x(t),t)=∂J^∂t(x(t),t)J^x(x(t),t)=∂J^∂x(x(t),t)(8)\begin{aligned} \hat{J}_t(x(t),t)&=\frac{\partial\hat{J}}{\partial{t}}(x(t),t)\\ \hat{J}_x(x(t),t)&=\frac{\partial\hat{J}}{\partial{x}}(x(t),t) \end{aligned} \tag{8} J^t​(x(t),t)J^x​(x(t),t)​=∂t∂J^​(x(t),t)=∂x∂J^​(x(t),t)​(8)

代入（7）中可以得到：

J^(x(t+Δt),t+Δt)≈J^(x(t),t)+J^t(x(t),t)Δt+J^x(x(t),t)[x(t+Δt)−x(t)]≈J^(x(t),t)+J^t(x(t),t)Δt+J^x(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)Δt(9)\begin{aligned} \hat{J}(x(t+\Delta{t}), t+\Delta{t})&\approx\hat{J}(x(t), t)+\hat{J}_t(x(t),t)\Delta{t}+\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)[x(t+\Delta{t})-x(t)]\\ &\approx\hat{J}(x(t), t)+\hat{J}_t(x(t),t)\Delta{t}+\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)\Delta{t}\\ \end{aligned} \tag{9} J^(x(t+Δt),t+Δt)​≈J^(x(t),t)+J^t​(x(t),t)Δt+J^x​(x(t),t)[x(t+Δt)−x(t)]≈J^(x(t),t)+J^t​(x(t),t)Δt+J^x​(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)Δt​(9)

将公式（10）和公式（9）代入到公式（6），得到：

J^(x(t),t)=min⁡u(t)∈U{g(x(t),u(t),t)Δt+J^(x(t),t)+J^t(x(t),t)Δt+J^x(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)Δt}=J^(x(t),t)+J^t(x(t),t)Δt+min⁡u(t)∈U{g(x(t),u(t),t)Δt+J^x(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)Δt}0=J^t(x(t),t)Δt+min⁡u(t)∈U{g(x(t),u(t),t)Δt+J^x(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)Δt}(11)\begin{aligned} \hat{J}(x(t), t)&=\underset{u(t)\in\mathscr{U}}{\min}\{g(x(t),u(t),t)\Delta{t}+\hat{J}(x(t),t)+\hat{J}_t(x(t),t)\Delta{t}+\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)\Delta{t}\}\\ &=\hat{J}(x(t),t)+\hat{J}_t(x(t),t)\Delta{t}+\underset{u(t)\in\mathscr{U}}{\min}\{g(x(t),u(t),t)\Delta{t}+\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)\Delta{t}\}\\ 0&=\hat{J}_t(x(t),t)\Delta{t}+\underset{u(t)\in\mathscr{U}}{\min}\{g(x(t),u(t),t)\Delta{t}+\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)\Delta{t}\} \end{aligned} \tag{11} J^(x(t),t)0​=u(t)∈Umin​{g(x(t),u(t),t)Δt+J^(x(t),t)+J^t​(x(t),t)Δt+J^x​(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)Δt}=J^(x(t),t)+J^t​(x(t),t)Δt+u(t)∈Umin​{g(x(t),u(t),t)Δt+J^x​(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)Δt}=J^t​(x(t),t)Δt+u(t)∈Umin​{g(x(t),u(t),t)Δt+J^x​(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)Δt}​(11)

上式中提取出和u(t)u(t)u(t)无关的项目，得到最终的结果。这是一个J^(x(t),t)\hat{J}(x(t),t)J^(x(t),t)的偏微分方程，根据最终状态J^(x(tf),tf)\hat{J}(x(t_f),t_f)J^(x(tf​),tf​)反向推导前边的状态，其中有：

J^(x(tf),tf)=h(x(tf))(12)\hat{J}(x(t_f),t_f)=h(x(t_f)) \tag{12} J^(x(tf​),tf​)=h(x(tf​))(12)

HJB(Hamiltonian-Jacobi-Bellman)等式

下面定义哈密顿量（Hamiltonian）：

H(x(t),u(t),J^x(x(t),t),t)=g(x(t),u(t),t)+J^x(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)(13)\mathscr{H}(x(t),u(t),\hat{J}_x(x(t),t),t)=g(x(t),u(t),t)+\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t) \tag{13} H(x(t),u(t),J^x​(x(t),t),t)=g(x(t),u(t),t)+J^x​(x(t),t)a(x(t),u(t),t)(13)

根据公式（11），代入公式（13），并且约掉Δt\Delta tΔt，得到：

−J^t(x(t),t)=min⁡u(t)∈U{H(x(t),u(t),J^x(x(t),t),t)}(14)\begin{aligned} -\hat{J}_t(x(t),t)&=\underset{u(t)\in\mathscr{U}}{\min}\{\mathscr{H}(x(t),u(t),\hat{J}_x(x(t),t),t)\} \end{aligned} \tag{14} −J^t​(x(t),t)​=u(t)∈Umin​{H(x(t),u(t),J^x​(x(t),t),t)}​(14)

这即是HJB等式。

连续时域LQR（Continuous LQR）

下面考虑如下线性系统模型（Linear System Model）和它的二次代价函数（Quadratic Cost Function）：

x˙(t)=A(t)x(t)+B(t)u(t)(15)\dot{x}(t)=A(t)x(t)+B(t)u(t) \tag{15} x˙(t)=A(t)x(t)+B(t)u(t)(15)

J=12x(tf)THx(tf)+12∫t0tf{x(t)TRxx(t)x(t)+u(t)TRuu(t)u(t)}dt(16)J=\frac{1}{2}x(t_f)^THx(t_f)+\frac{1}{2}\int_{t_0}^{t_f}\{x(t)^TR_{xx}(t)x(t)+u(t)^TR_{uu}(t)u(t)\}dt \tag{16} J=21​x(tf​)THx(tf​)+21​∫t0​tf​​{x(t)TRxx​(t)x(t)+u(t)TRuu​(t)u(t)}dt(16)

假设tft_ftf​是固定的，u(t)u(t)u(t)没有约束；假设H,Rxx≥0H,R_{xx}\geq 0H,Rxx​≥0(半正定)，Ruu(t)>0R_{uu}(t)>0Ruu​(t)>0（正定），根据公式（16）和公式（13）的定义，可以得到哈密顿量：

H(x(t),u(t),J^x(x(t),t),t)=12[x(t)TRxx(t)x(t)+u(t)TRuu(t)u(t)]+J^x(x(t),t)[A(t)x(t)+B(t)u(t)](17)\mathscr{H}(x(t),u(t),\hat{J}_x(x(t),t),t)=\frac{1}{2}[x(t)^TR_{xx}(t)x(t)+u(t)^TR_{uu}(t)u(t)]+\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)[A(t)x(t)+B(t)u(t)] \tag{17} H(x(t),u(t),J^x​(x(t),t),t)=21​[x(t)TRxx​(t)x(t)+u(t)TRuu​(t)u(t)]+J^x​(x(t),t)[A(t)x(t)+B(t)u(t)](17)

根据公式（14），需要找到一个最优的u(t)u(t)u(t)，即u^(t)\hat u(t)u^(t) ，使得H\mathscr{H}H最小，那么在没有约束的情况下，需要满足以下必要条件：

∂H∂u=u(t)Ruu(t)+J^x(x(t),t)B(t)=0(18)\frac{\partial\mathscr{H}}{\partial{u}}=u(t)R_{uu}(t)+\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)B(t)=0 \tag{18} ∂u∂H​=u(t)Ruu​(t)+J^x​(x(t),t)B(t)=0(18)

于是，根据上式可以得到一个最优控制率（Optimal Control Law）:

u^(t)=−Ruu−1(t)B(t)TJ^x(x(t),t)T(19)\hat{u}(t)=-R_{uu}^{-1}(t)B(t)^T\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)^T \tag{19} u^(t)=−Ruu−1​(t)B(t)TJ^x​(x(t),t)T(19)

如果需要使上述最优条件充分且必要，还需要满足：

∂2H∂u2=Ruu(t)>0(20)\frac{\partial^2\mathscr{H}}{\partial{u}^2}=R_{uu}(t)>0 \tag{20} ∂u2∂2H​=Ruu​(t)>0(20)

显然是成立的，因此该最优值是全局最小值。

于是根据（14）式，我们可以得到如下关系：

−J^t(x(t),t)=12x(t)TRxx(t)x(t)+J^x(x(t),t)A(t)x(t)−12J^x(x(t),t)B(t)Ruu−1(t)B(t)TJ^x(x(t),t)T(22)\begin{aligned} -\hat{J}_t(x(t),t)=\frac{1}{2}x(t)^TR_{xx}(t)x(t)+\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)A(t)x(t)-\frac{1}{2}\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)B(t)R_{uu}^{-1}(t)B(t)^T\hat{J}_x(x(t),t)^T \end{aligned} \tag{22} −J^t​(x(t),t)=21​x(t)TRxx​(t)x(t)+J^x​(x(t),t)A(t)x(t)−21​J^x​(x(t),t)B(t)Ruu−1​(t)B(t)TJ^x​(x(t),t)T​(22)

这是一个关于J^\hat JJ^的偏微分方程，根据（16）可以很容易的知道J^\hat JJ^的边界条件：

J^(x(tf),tf)=12xT(tf)Hx(tf)(23)\hat{J}(x(t_f),t_f)=\frac{1}{2}x^T(t_f)Hx(t_f) \tag{23} J^(x(tf​),tf​)=21​xT(tf​)Hx(tf​)(23)

根据上述公式，我们可以大胆假设J^\hat JJ^在所有时间ttt上均是x(t)x(t)x(t)的二次型（Quadratic Form），因此我们假设：

J^(x(t),t)=12xT(t)P(t)x(t),P(t)=PT(t)(24)\hat{J}(x(t),t)=\frac{1}{2}x^T(t)P(t)x(t), \quad P(t)=P^T(t) \tag{24} J^(x(t),t)=21​xT(t)P(t)x(t),P(t)=PT(t)(24)

根据假设很容易得到：
