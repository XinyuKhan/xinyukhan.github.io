强化学习理论基础(2)定理(1)Bellman方程

Last updated on 2026-04-20T23:42:43+08:00

强化学习理论基础-定理-Bellman方程

本文将推导Bellman方程在RL中的几种形式

1. 将QπQ_\piQπ​表示成QπQ_\piQπ​的递归形式

Qπ(st,at)=ESt+1,At+1[Rt+γQπ(St+1,At+1)∣St=st,At=at](1)\begin{aligned} Q_\pi(s_t, a_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}}[R_t + \gamma Q_\pi(S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {1} Qπ​(st​,at​)​=ESt+1​,At+1​​[Rt​+γQπ​(St+1​,At+1​)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​(1)

证明

根据定义

Qπ(st,at)=ESt+1:,At+1:[Ut∣St=st,At=at](1.1)\begin{aligned} Q_\pi(s_t, a_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{\mathcal{S}_{t+1:}, \mathcal{A}_{t+1:}}[U_t | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {1.1} Qπ​(st​,at​)​=ESt+1:​,At+1:​​[Ut​∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​(1.1)

其中

Ut=Rt+γ∑k=t+1nγk−t−1Rk⏟=Ut+1=Rt+γUt+1(1.2)\begin{aligned} U_t &= R_t + \gamma \underbrace{\sum_{k=t+1}^{n} \gamma^{k-t-1} R_k}_{=U_{t+1}} \\ &= R_t + \gamma U_{t+1} \\ \end{aligned} \tag {1.2} Ut​​=Rt​+γ=Ut+1​k=t+1∑n​γk−t−1Rk​​​=Rt​+γUt+1​​(1.2)

代入可得到

Qπ(st,at)=ESt+1:,At+1:[Rt+γUt+1∣St=st,At=at]=ESt+1:,At+1:[Rt∣St=st,At=at]⏟(1.3.1)+γESt+1:,At+1:[Ut+1∣St=st,At=at]⏟(1.3.2)(1.3)\begin{aligned} Q_\pi(s_t, a_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{\mathcal{S}_{t+1:}, \mathcal{A}_{t+1:}}[R_t + \gamma U_{t+1} | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ & = \underbrace{\mathbb{E}_{\mathcal{S}_{t+1:}, \mathcal{A}_{t+1:}}[R_t | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t]}_{(1.3.1)} + \gamma \underbrace{\mathbb{E}_{\mathcal{S}_{t+1:}, \mathcal{A}_{t+1:}}[U_{t+1} | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t]}_{(1.3.2)} \\ \end{aligned} \tag {1.3} Qπ​(st​,at​)​=ESt+1:​,At+1:​​[Rt​+γUt+1​∣St​=st​,At​=at​]=(1.3.1)ESt+1:​,At+1:​​[Rt​∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​​+γ(1.3.2)ESt+1:​,At+1:​​[Ut+1​∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​​​(1.3)

其中1.3.1中的RtR_tRt​是在智能体以状态StS_tSt​作为输入应用策略π\piπ得到动作AtA_tAt​后，环境反馈转移到状态St+1S_{t+1}St+1​的时候获得的奖励。因此其随机性来源于StS_tSt​，AtA_tAt​和St+1S_{t+1}St+1​，1.3.1中StS_tSt​和AtA_tAt​都已固定为sts_tst​和ata_tat​，因此1.3.1的随机性仅来源于St+1S_{t+1}St+1​，于是有：

(1.3.1)=ESt+1[Rt∣St=st,At=at](1.4)\begin{aligned} (1.3.1) & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}}[R_t | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {1.4} (1.3.1)​=ESt+1​​[Rt​∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​(1.4)

1.3.2则可以写成如下形式：

(1.3.2)=ESt+1,At+1[ESt+2:,At+2:[Ut+1∣St+1,At+1]∣St=st,At=at]=ESt+1,At+1[Qπ(St+1,At+1)∣St=st,At=at](1.5)\begin{aligned} (1.3.2) & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}}[\mathbb{E}_{\mathcal{S}_{t+2:}, \mathcal{A}_{t+2:}}[U_{t+1} | S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}] | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}}[Q_\pi(S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {1.5} (1.3.2)​=ESt+1​,At+1​​[ESt+2:​,At+2:​​[Ut+1​∣St+1​,At+1​]∣St​=st​,At​=at​]=ESt+1​,At+1​​[Qπ​(St+1​,At+1​)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​(1.5)

将(1.4)和(1.5)代入(1.3)中可得公式1。

2. 将QπQ_\piQπ​表示成VπV_\piVπ​的递归形式

Qπ(st,at)=ESt+1[Rt+γVπ(St+1)∣St=st,At=at](2)\begin{aligned} Q_\pi(s_t, a_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}}[R_t + \gamma V_\pi(S_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {2} Qπ​(st​,at​)​=ESt+1​​[Rt​+γVπ​(St+1​)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​(2)

证明

根据定义

Vπ(st)=EAt+1[Qπ(st,At+1)∣St=st](2.1)\begin{aligned} V_\pi(s_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{A_{t+1}}[Q_\pi(s_t, A_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {2.1} Vπ​(st​)​=EAt+1​​[Qπ​(st​,At+1​)∣St​=st​]​(2.1)

代入公式1

Qπ(st,at)=ESt+1[Rt∣St=st,At=at]+γESt+1,At+1[Qπ(St+1,At+1)∣St=st,At=at]=ESt+1[Rt∣St=st,At=at]+γESt+1[EAt+1[Qπ(St+1,At+1)]∣St=st,At=at]=ESt+1[Rt∣St=st,At=at]+γESt+1[Vπ(St+1)∣St=st,At=at]=ESt+1[Rt+γVπ(St+1)∣St=st,At=at](2.2)\begin{aligned} Q_\pi(s_t, a_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}}[R_t | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] + \gamma \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}}[Q_\pi(S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}}[R_t | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] + \gamma \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}}[\mathbb{E}_{A_{t+1}}[Q_\pi(S_{t+1}, A_{t+1})]| S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}}[R_t | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] + \gamma \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}}[V_\pi(S_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}}[R_t + \gamma V_\pi(S_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {2.2} Qπ​(st​,at​)​=ESt+1​​[Rt​∣St​=st​,At​=at​]+γESt+1​,At+1​​[Qπ​(St+1​,At+1​)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]=ESt+1​​[Rt​∣St​=st​,At​=at​]+γESt+1​​[EAt+1​​[Qπ​(St+1​,At+1​)]∣St​=st​,At​=at​]=ESt+1​​[Rt​∣St​=st​,At​=at​]+γESt+1​​[Vπ​(St+1​)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]=ESt+1​​[Rt​+γVπ​(St+1​)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​(2.2)

3. 将VπV_\piVπ​表示成VπV_\piVπ​的递归形式

Vπ(st)=EAt,St+1[Rt+γVπ(St+1)∣St=st](3)\begin{aligned} V_\pi(s_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{A_t, S_{t+1}}[R_t + \gamma V_\pi(S_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {3} Vπ​(st​)​=EAt​,St+1​​[Rt​+γVπ​(St+1​)∣St​=st​]​(3)

证明

根据定义

Vπ(st)=EAt[Qπ(st,At)](3.1)\begin{aligned} V_\pi(s_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{A_t}[Q_\pi(s_t, A_t)] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {3.1} Vπ​(st​)​=EAt​​[Qπ​(st​,At​)]​(3.1)

代入公式2

Vπ(st)=EAt[ESt+1[Rt+γVπ(St+1)∣St=st,At=at]]=EAt,St+1[Rt+γVπ(St+1)∣St=st](3.2)\begin{aligned} V_\pi(s_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{A_t}[ \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}}[R_t + \gamma V_\pi(S_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] ] \\ & = \mathbb{E}_{A_t, S_{t+1}}[R_t + \gamma V_\pi(S_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {3.2} Vπ​(st​)​=EAt​​[ESt+1​​[Rt​+γVπ​(St+1​)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]]=EAt​,St+1​​[Rt​+γVπ​(St+1​)∣St​=st​]​(3.2)

4. 最优Bellman方程

Q∗(st,at)=ESt+1∼p(⋅∣st,at)[Rt+γmax⁡A∈AQ∗(St+1,A)∣St=st,At=at](4)\begin{aligned} Q_*(s_t, a_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1} \sim p(\cdot |s_t, a_t) }[R_t + \gamma \max_{A \in \mathcal{A}} Q_*(S_{t+1}, A) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {4} Q∗​(st​,at​)​=ESt+1​∼p(⋅∣st​,at​)​[Rt​+γA∈Amax​Q∗​(St+1​,A)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​(4)

证明

最优策略函数定义如下：

π∗(a∣s)=arg⁡max⁡πQπ(s,a),∀s∈S,a∈A(4.1)\begin{aligned} \pi^*(a|s) & = \arg\max_\pi Q_\pi(s, a), \forall s \in \mathcal{S}, a \in \mathcal{A} \\ \end{aligned} \tag {4.1} π∗(a∣s)​=argπmax​Qπ​(s,a),∀s∈S,a∈A​(4.1)

由Bellman方程公式1可得

Qπ∗(st,at)=ESt+1,At+1[Rt+γQπ∗(St+1,At+1)∣St=st,At=at](4.2)\begin{aligned} Q_{\pi^*}(s_t, a_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}}[R_t + \gamma Q_{\pi^*}(S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {4.2} Qπ∗​(st​,at​)​=ESt+1​,At+1​​[Rt​+γQπ∗​(St+1​,At+1​)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​(4.2)

根据定义，最优状态-动作值函数Q∗(st,at)Q_*(s_t, a_t)Q∗​(st​,at​)是所有策略的最大值，因此有

Q∗(st,at)≜Qπ∗(st,at)(4.3)\begin{aligned} Q_*(s_t, a_t) & \triangleq Q_{\pi^*}(s_t, a_t) \\ \end{aligned} \tag {4.3} Q∗​(st​,at​)​≜Qπ∗​(st​,at​)​(4.3)

于是有

Q∗(st,at)=ESt+1,At+1[Rt+γQ∗(St+1,At+1)∣St=st,At=at](4.4)\begin{aligned} Q_*(s_t, a_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}}[R_t + \gamma Q_*(S_{t+1}, A_{t+1}) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \end{aligned} \tag {4.4} Q∗​(st​,at​)​=ESt+1​,At+1​​[Rt​+γQ∗​(St+1​,At+1​)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​(4.4)

其中At+1A_{t+1}At+1​是由最优策略π∗\pi^*π∗根据状态St+1S_{t+1}St+1​选择的动作，因此有

At+1=arg⁡max⁡A∈AQ∗(St+1,A)(4.5)\begin{aligned} A_{t+1} & = \arg\max_{A \in \mathcal{A}} Q_*(S_{t+1}, A) \\ \end{aligned} \tag {4.5} At+1​​=argA∈Amax​Q∗​(St+1​,A)​(4.5)

At+1A_{t+1}At+1​是状态St+1S_{t+1}St+1​的确定性函数，因此公式4.4可以写成

Q∗(st,at)=ESt+1∼p(⋅∣st,at)[Rt+γmax⁡A∈AQ∗(St+1,A)∣St=st,At=at]\begin{aligned} Q_*(s_t, a_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{S_{t+1} \sim p(\cdot |s_t, a_t)}[R_t + \gamma \max_{A \in \mathcal{A}} Q_*(S_{t+1}, A) | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \end{aligned} Q∗​(st​,at​)​=ESt+1​∼p(⋅∣st​,at​)​[Rt​+γA∈Amax​Q∗​(St+1​,A)∣St​=st​,At​=at​]​
