强化学习理论基础(3)算法(4)REINFORCE算法

强化学习理论基础-算法-REINFORCE算法

在《策略梯度定理》一文中我们证明推导了策略梯度定理，现在把它抄到下边以供回忆

∂J(θ)∂θ=1−γn1−γES∼d(⋅)[EA∼π(⋅∣S;θ)[∂ln⁡π(A∣S;θ)∂θ⋅Qπ(S,A)]](0.1)\begin{aligned} \frac{\partial J(\boldsymbol{\theta})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}} &= \frac{1 - \gamma^n}{1 - \gamma} \mathbb{E}_{S \sim d(\cdot)} \left [\mathbb{E}_{A \sim \pi(\cdot | S; \boldsymbol{\theta})} \left[ \frac{\partial \ln \pi(A | S; \boldsymbol{\theta})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}} \cdot Q_{\pi}(S, A) \right]\right] \end{aligned} \tag {0.1} ∂θ∂J(θ)​​=1−γ1−γn​ES∼d(⋅)​[EA∼π(⋅∣S;θ)​[∂θ∂lnπ(A∣S;θ)​⋅Qπ​(S,A)]]​(0.1)

同时我们介绍了近似策略梯度，即使用蒙特卡洛近似表示上述期望，得到一个随机策略梯度函数ggg

g(s,a;θ)≜∂ln⁡π(a∣s;θ)∂θ⋅Qπ(s,a)(0.2)\begin{aligned} \boldsymbol{g}(s, a; \boldsymbol{\theta}) &\triangleq \frac{\partial \ln \pi(a | s; \boldsymbol{\theta})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}} \cdot Q_{\pi}(s, a) \end{aligned} \tag{0.2} g(s,a;θ)​≜∂θ∂lnπ(a∣s;θ)​⋅Qπ​(s,a)​(0.2)

接下来我们使用上述方法来推导REINFORCE算法。

1. 推导

回忆《策略梯度定理》中的公式(2.3.1)，抄在下方

∂J(θ)∂θ=∂∂θES1∼d(⋅)[Vπ(S1)]=ES1∼d(⋅)[∂Vπ(S1)∂θ]=ES1,A1[g(S1,A1;θ)]+γES1,A1,S2,A2[g(S2,A2;θ)]+γ2ES1,A1,S2,A2,S3,A3[g(S3,A3;θ)]+⋯+γn−1ES1,A1,S2,…,Sn,An[g(Sn,An;θ)](1.1)\begin{aligned} \frac{\partial J(\boldsymbol{\theta})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}} =& \frac{\partial}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}} \mathbb{E}_{S_1 \sim d(\cdot)}\left[V_\pi(S_1)\right] \\ =& \mathbb{E}_{S_1 \sim d(\cdot)}\left[\frac{\partial V_\pi(S_1)}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}}\right] \\ =& \mathbb{E}_{S_1,A_1}\left[\boldsymbol{g}(S_1, A_1;\boldsymbol{\theta})\right] \\ &+ \gamma \mathbb{E}_{S_1,A_1,S_2,A_2}\left[\boldsymbol{g}(S_2, A_2;\boldsymbol{\theta})\right] \\ &+ \gamma^2 \mathbb{E}_{S_1,A_1,S_2,A_2,S_3,A_3}\left[\boldsymbol{g}(S_3, A_3;\boldsymbol{\theta})\right] \\ &+ \cdots \\ &+ \gamma^{n-1} \mathbb{E}_{S_1,A_1,S_2,\ldots,S_n,A_n}\left[\boldsymbol{g}(S_n, A_n;\boldsymbol{\theta})\right] \\ \end{aligned} \tag{1.1} ∂θ∂J(θ)​===​∂θ∂​ES1​∼d(⋅)​[Vπ​(S1​)]ES1​∼d(⋅)​[∂θ∂Vπ​(S1​)​]ES1​,A1​​[g(S1​,A1​;θ)]+γES1​,A1​,S2​,A2​​[g(S2​,A2​;θ)]+γ2ES1​,A1​,S2​,A2​,S3​,A3​​[g(S3​,A3​;θ)]+⋯+γn−1ES1​,A1​,S2​,…,Sn​,An​​[g(Sn​,An​;θ)]​(1.1)

对上述期望做蒙特卡洛近似，假设我们从状态s1s_1s1​开始，应用策略 π(a∣s;θnow)\pi(a \mid s; \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now})π(a∣s;θnow​)控制智能体和环境交互，产生如下轨迹

s1,a1,r1,  s2,a2,r2,  …,  sn,an,rn(1.2)\begin{aligned} s_1, a_1, r_1,\space\space s_2, a_2, r_2, \space\space \ldots, \space\space s_n, a_n, r_n \end{aligned} \tag{1.2} s1​,a1​,r1​,  s2​,a2​,r2​,  …,  sn​,an​,rn​​(1.2)

使用上述轨迹对公式(1.1)进行蒙特卡洛近似，得到

∂J(θnow)∂θnow≈g(s1,a1;θnow)+γg(s2,a2;θnow)+γ2g(s3,a3;θnow)+⋯+γn−1g(sn,an;θnow)(1.3)\begin{aligned} \frac{\partial J(\boldsymbol{\theta}_{now})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now}} \approx g(s_1, a_1; \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now}) + \gamma g(s_2, a_2; \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now}) + \gamma^2 g(s_3, a_3; \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now}) + \cdots + \gamma^{n-1} g(s_n, a_n; \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now}) \end{aligned} \tag{1.3} ∂θnow​∂J(θnow​)​≈g(s1​,a1​;θnow​)+γg(s2​,a2​;θnow​)+γ2g(s3​,a3​;θnow​)+⋯+γn−1g(sn​,an​;θnow​)​(1.3)

根据定义有

ut=rt+γrt+1+γ2rt+2+⋯+γn−trn=∑i=tnγi−tri(1.4)\begin{aligned} u_t & = r_t + \gamma r_{t+1} + \gamma^2 r_{t+2} + \cdots + \gamma^{n-t} r_n = \sum_{i=t}^{n} \gamma^{i-t} r_i \\ \end{aligned} \tag{1.4} ut​​=rt​+γrt+1​+γ2rt+2​+⋯+γn−trn​=i=t∑n​γi−tri​​(1.4)

我们可以使用utu_tut​近似公式(0.2)中的 Qπ(s,a)Q_{\pi}(s, a)Qπ​(s,a)，有

g(st,at;θnow)≈∂ln⁡π(at∣st;θnow)∂θnow⋅ut(1.5)\begin{aligned} g(s_t, a_t; \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now}) & \approx \frac{\partial \ln \pi(a_t | s_t; \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now}} \cdot u_t \\ \end{aligned} \tag{1.5} g(st​,at​;θnow​)​≈∂θnow​∂lnπ(at​∣st​;θnow​)​⋅ut​​(1.5)

代入公式(1.3)，我们得到

∂J(θnow)∂θnow≈∑t=1n(γt−1⋅∂ln⁡π(at∣st;θnow)∂θnow⋅ut)(1.6)\begin{aligned} \frac{\partial J(\boldsymbol{\theta}_{now})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now}} & \approx \sum_{t=1}^{n} \left( \gamma^{t-1} \cdot \frac{\partial \ln \pi(a_t | s_t; \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now}} \cdot u_t \right) \end{aligned} \tag{1.6} ∂θnow​∂J(θnow​)​​≈t=1∑n​(γt−1⋅∂θnow​∂lnπ(at​∣st​;θnow​)​⋅ut​)​(1.6)

于是根据随机梯度上升公式，我们更新参数

θnew←θnow+α⋅∑t=1n(γt−1⋅∂ln⁡π(at∣st;θnow)∂θnow⋅ut)(1.7)\begin{aligned} \boldsymbol{\theta}_{new} & \leftarrow \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now} + \alpha \cdot \sum_{t=1}^{n} \left( \gamma^{t-1} \cdot \frac{\partial \ln \pi(a_t | s_t; \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}_{now}} \cdot u_t \right) \end{aligned} \tag{1.7} θnew​​←θnow​+α⋅t=1∑n​(γt−1⋅∂θnow​∂lnπ(at​∣st​;θnow​)​⋅ut​)​(1.7)
