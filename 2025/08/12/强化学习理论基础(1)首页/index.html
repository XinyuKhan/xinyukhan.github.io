定义

finite-horizon 回报:

Ut=Rt+γRt+1+γ2Rt+2+⋯+γn−tRn=∑i=tnγi−tRiut=rt+γrt+1+γ2rt+2+⋯+γn−trn=∑i=tnγi−tri\begin{aligned} U_t & = R_t + \gamma R_{t+1} + \gamma^2 R_{t+2} + \cdots + \gamma^{n-t} R_n = \sum_{i=t}^{n} \gamma^{i-t} R_i \\ u_t & = r_t + \gamma r_{t+1} + \gamma^2 r_{t+2} + \cdots + \gamma^{n-t} r_n = \sum_{i=t}^{n} \gamma^{i-t} r_i \\ \end{aligned} Ut​ut​​=Rt​+γRt+1​+γ2Rt+2​+⋯+γn−tRn​=i=t∑n​γi−tRi​=rt​+γrt+1​+γ2rt+2​+⋯+γn−trn​=i=t∑n​γi−tri​​

infinite-horizon 回报:

Ut=Rt+γRt+1+γ2Rt+2+⋯=∑i=t∞γi−tRiut=rt+γrt+1+γ2rt+2+⋯=∑i=t∞γi−tri\begin{aligned} U_t & = R_t + \gamma R_{t+1} + \gamma^2 R_{t+2} + \cdots = \sum_{i=t}^{\infty} \gamma^{i-t} R_i \\ u_t & = r_t + \gamma r_{t+1} + \gamma^2 r_{t+2} + \cdots = \sum_{i=t}^{\infty} \gamma^{i-t} r_i \\ \end{aligned} Ut​ut​​=Rt​+γRt+1​+γ2Rt+2​+⋯=i=t∑∞​γi−tRi​=rt​+γrt+1​+γ2rt+2​+⋯=i=t∑∞​γi−tri​​

状态-动作值函数:

\begin{aligned} Q_\pi(s_t, a_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{\mathcal{S}_{t+1:}, \mathcal{A}_{t+1:}}[U_t | S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t] \\ \mathcal{S}_{t+1:} &= \set{S_{t+1}, S_{t+2}, \ldots} \\ \mathcal{A}_{t+1:} &= \set{A_{t+1}, A_{t+2}, \ldots} \\ \end{aligned}

最优状态-动作值函数:

Q∗(st,at)=max⁡πQπ(st,at),∀st∈S,at∈A\begin{aligned} Q_*(s_t, a_t) & = \max_\pi Q_\pi(s_t, a_t), \forall s_t \in \mathcal{S}, a_t \in \mathcal{A} \\ \end{aligned} Q∗​(st​,at​)​=πmax​Qπ​(st​,at​),∀st​∈S,at​∈A​

状态值函数:

Vπ(st)=EA∼π[Qπ(st,A)]\begin{aligned} V_\pi(s_t) & = \mathbb{E}_{A\sim\pi}[Q_\pi(s_t, A)] \\ \end{aligned} Vπ​(st​)​=EA∼π​[Qπ​(st​,A)]​
